# Getting Started with EarnedLab

EarnedLab is an AI business operating system for entrepreneurs, solo founders, and small-business owners. This guide covers account creation, the trial period, and the first steps to launching your business on the platform.

---

## Creating an account

1. Go to [earnedlab.com/signup](https://earnedlab.com/signup).
2. Enter your name, email address, and a password.
3. Verify your email — a confirmation link is sent to the address you provided. Click it to activate your account.

Email verification is required before you can access the platform. If you don't see the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or use the "Resend verification" option on the sign-in page.

---

## The 7-day free trial

Every new account starts with a 7-day free trial. During the trial you have access to:

- The discovery questionnaire and business matching
- Market validation and business planning
- The intelligence assistant
- The content lab
- The marketing agent (up to 3 full analyses)
- The management agent (up to 1 implementation)
- Website creation and live deployment

The trial has the same daily AI token budget as the Starter plan: 20,000 tokens per day, resetting at midnight UTC. If you hit the daily limit, it resets the following day.

No credit card is required to start the trial. After 7 days, the account moves to a read-only view — your data is preserved and you can subscribe at any time to resume.

---

## Step 1 — Discovery

The first thing you do in EarnedLab is the discovery questionnaire. It takes 5–8 minutes and covers:

- **Skills and background** — Your professional experience and what you already know how to do well.
- **Available time** — Hours per week you can realistically commit to a side business.
- **Starting budget** — How much you can invest in getting started (can be $0).
- **Income goals** — What you need the business to generate and on what timeline.
- **Location** — Whether your business would be local, remote, or online-only.

Based on your answers, EarnedLab generates 3–5 matched business directions, each with:

- A fit score based on your inputs
- Estimated startup cost
- Estimated time to first revenue
- Risk level (low / medium / high)
- A brief explanation of why it matches your situation

Select the direction you want to develop further.

---

## Step 2 — Validation

Once you've selected a business direction, run a market validation. The marketing agent produces a full analysis:

- **Target customer profile** — Who the customer is, what problem they have, and what they'll pay.
- **Market demand** — Size and demand signals for your specific direction and location.
- **Competitive landscape** — Who else is serving this market, their positioning, and how to differentiate.
- **Pricing benchmarks** — What comparable services or products charge.
- **Risk factors** — The top risks and mitigation strategies.

Review the validation before committing time or investment. If the market doesn't support the idea, you can go back and select a different direction.

---

## Step 3 — Planning

EarnedLab converts your validated business direction into a structured plan:

- **Offer definition** — Your service, scope, and pricing model.
- **Brand basics** — A business name, positioning statement, and target customer description.
- **Website structure** — The pages your site needs and the content for each.
- **30-day task list** — Specific, ordered tasks with milestone markers.

Review each section and edit anything that doesn't fit. The plan is yours — agents produce a starting point, and you refine it.

---

## Step 4 — Launch

EarnedLab generates and deploys your business website. The process:

1. Review the AI-generated website content (headline, services, about section, contact).
2. Approve and deploy — EarnedLab deploys the site to a Netlify-hosted URL.
3. Connect a custom domain if you have one, or use the generated URL.

The live website is connected to the management agent, which can update it as your business evolves.

---

## Step 5 — The hub

After launch, your business lives in the hub — your central workspace:

- **Task management** — Track tasks from the plan plus anything you add. Agent-generated tasks appear automatically.
- **Revenue and lead tracking** — Log entries and see trends.
- **Business health** — A summary of your current status at a glance.
- **Marketing agent** — Run analyses on demand (or automatically on Autopilot).
- **Management agent** — Review and approve implementation tasks.
- **Content lab** — Generate social media, email, and web content.
- **Intelligence assistant** — Ask anything about your business.
- **Integrations** — Connect channels for the management agent to use.

---

## Daily AI limits

All plans include a daily AI usage budget that resets at midnight UTC.

| Plan | Daily budget |
|------|-------------|
| Trial | 20,000 tokens |
| Starter | 20,000 tokens |
| Pro | 110,000 tokens |
| Pro Autopilot | 110,000 tokens |

When the daily budget is reached, AI-powered features (marketing agent, management agent, content lab, intelligence assistant) pause until the budget resets. The rest of the platform — task management, revenue tracking, and your existing data — remains fully accessible.
