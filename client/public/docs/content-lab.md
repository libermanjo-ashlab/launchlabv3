# Content Lab

The content lab generates channel-specific content on demand — social media captions, image generation, email drafts, website copy, and full campaign content. It is available on all plans.

---

## What the content lab generates

### Social media content
- **Instagram** — Caption, hashtag set, and image prompt optimized for engagement.
- **Twitter / X** — Tweet and optional thread expansion.
- **LinkedIn** — Professional post in short or long form.
- **TikTok** — Script outline, caption, and on-screen text suggestions.
- **Facebook** — Post copy for page distribution.

### Images
- AI-generated images via DALL-E, created alongside social captions or independently.
- Provide a description or let EarnedLab generate based on your brand and content angle.

### Email content
- **Campaign emails** — Subject line, preheader, body, and call to action for one-time campaigns.
- **Newsletter drafts** — Full newsletter structure with sections and body content.
- **Welcome or nurture sequences** — Multi-email sequences with consistent tone and progression.

### Website copy
- **Landing page sections** — Hero, value proposition, service descriptions, testimonial placeholders, calls to action.
- **About page** — Brand story and team intro tailored to your business profile.
- **Service or product pages** — Descriptions, features, and pricing copy.

### Campaign content
- **Full campaign brief** — A coordinated set of content across multiple channels for a single campaign theme, including post copy, email, and website updates.

---

## How to use the content lab

1. Open your business hub.
2. Navigate to **Content Lab**.
3. Select the content type: social post, image, email, website copy, or campaign.
4. Choose the channel (for social content) and describe the goal or topic, or let EarnedLab suggest based on your latest marketing analysis.
5. Review and edit the generated content.
6. Use it directly, pass it to the management agent for publication, or download/copy for use outside the platform.

---

## Brand voice

The content lab uses your business profile — your offer, target customer, positioning statement, and tone — as the default context. Content is generated to match your brand rather than a generic template.

If you want to adjust the tone for a specific piece:
- Add a note when generating: "formal," "casual," "humorous," "direct."
- Edit the generated content before publishing or passing to the management agent.

---

## Integration with the management agent

Content generated in the content lab can be passed directly to the management agent for publication:

1. Generate content in the content lab.
2. Review and approve.
3. Click **Send to management agent** — the action is queued.
4. On Pro, approve the implementation. On Pro Autopilot, it executes automatically if the action type is pre-authorized.

## Token usage

Content lab requests use approximately 800–2,500 tokens depending on the content type:

| Content type | Estimated tokens |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Single social post | ~800 |
| Email draft | ~1,500 |
| Campaign brief | ~2,500 |
| Website section | ~1,200 |
| Image generation | Separate DALL-E usage (not counted against token budget) |

---

## Plan access

The content lab is included on all plans: Trial, Starter, Pro, and Pro Autopilot. Usage counts against the daily AI token budget.
