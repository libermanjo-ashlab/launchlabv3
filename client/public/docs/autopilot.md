# Autopilot Mode

Pro Autopilot is EarnedLab's fully autonomous operating mode. The marketing agent and management agent run on a schedule without manual input, executing within the boundaries you define. It is available on the Pro Autopilot plan ($199/month).

---

## How autopilot works

On standard Pro, you trigger agents manually and approve each action before it runs.

On Pro Autopilot:

1. The marketing agent runs automatically on your configured schedule (daily, every few days, or weekly).
2. The marketing agent produces insights and recommendations.
3. For action categories you've pre-authorized, the management agent executes automatically — no approval step required.
4. For action categories that aren't pre-authorized, the recommendations are queued for your review.
5. Everything is logged in your hub.

You define what the agents are authorized to do autonomously. Actions outside those boundaries don't run without your approval.

---

## What can run automatically

You configure autopilot by selecting which action categories the management agent can execute without per-action approval:

- **Social media posts** — Publish agent-generated captions and images to connected social channels.
- **Website updates** — Apply copy improvements and new sections to your live Netlify site.
- **Email campaigns** — Send campaigns to connected email lists.
- **Google Business updates** — Publish updates, offers, and posts to your Google Business Profile.

You can enable any combination of these. For example, you might enable automatic social posting but require manual approval for website changes.

---

## Configuring autopilot

1. From your business hub, go to **Settings > Autopilot**.
2. Toggle autopilot on.
3. Set the marketing agent schedule (daily / every 3 days / weekly).
4. For each action category, select:
   - **Automatic** — management agent executes without review
   - **Queue for review** — agent generates the action plan, you approve before it runs
5. Save your configuration.

You can change autopilot settings at any time. Changes take effect on the next scheduled run.

---

## Daily token budget on Autopilot

Pro Autopilot uses the same 110,000 token/day budget as Pro. Automated runs count against this budget.

A typical autopilot cycle uses approximately:

- Marketing analysis: ~12,000 tokens
- 3–5 social posts: ~4,500 tokens (estimated)
- 1 website update: ~1,800 tokens (estimated)

If the daily budget is reached mid-run, the current cycle pauses and resumes the next day.

---

## Monitoring autopilot activity

Everything the agents do in autopilot mode is logged in your hub:

- **Autopilot log** — A chronological list of every automated action, with content, channel, timestamp, and outcome.
- **Email summaries** — Optional daily or weekly email summaries of what autopilot ran and what the outcomes were.
- **Alerts** — Notifications when an action fails or a channel becomes disconnected.

You can pause autopilot at any time from the hub. Pausing does not affect existing connected channels or previously published content.

---

## Pause and resume

- **Pause** — Stops scheduled runs. Queued reviews remain accessible.
- **Resume** — Scheduled runs restart on their next scheduled time.
- **Reset** — Clears the current schedule and queue. Does not affect past actions or published content.

---

## Plan requirement

Autopilot mode requires the **Pro Autopilot** plan ($199/month). It is not available on Starter or standard Pro plans.
