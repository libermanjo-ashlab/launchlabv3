# Plans and Daily Limits

EarnedLab has four plans: a 7-day free trial and three paid tiers. All plans include a daily AI usage budget that resets at midnight UTC.

---

## Plans

### Free Trial — $0 for 7 days
- Full access to discovery, validation, and planning
- Marketing agent: up to 3 full analyses
- Management agent: up to 1 implementation
- Content lab: included
- Intelligence assistant: included
- Website creation and deployment: included
- Daily AI token budget: 20,000 tokens
- No credit card required

After 7 days, the account moves to read-only mode. Your data is preserved. Subscribe at any time to resume.

---

### Starter — $39/month
- Discovery, validation, and planning: included
- Marketing agent: unlimited analyses (within daily budget)
- Management agent: not included
- Content lab: included
- Intelligence assistant: included
- Website access: included (no automated updates without management agent)
- Daily AI token budget: 20,000 tokens
- Revenue and lead tracking: included

The Starter plan is for founders who want marketing intelligence and content generation without automated implementation.

---

### Pro — $89/month
- Everything in Starter
- Management agent: included — implements recommendations with per-action approval
- Autopilot mode: not included
- Daily AI token budget: 110,000 tokens
- All integrations available

The Pro plan is for founders who want the management agent to implement recommendations — website updates, social posts, email campaigns — with manual approval for each action.

---

### Pro Autopilot — $199/month
- Everything in Pro
- Autopilot mode: included — agents run on a schedule, management agent executes pre-approved action categories automatically
- Daily AI token budget: 110,000 tokens
- All integrations available

The Pro Autopilot plan is for founders who want the platform to run on its own within defined boundaries.

## Daily AI token budget

All AI-powered features — the marketing agent, management agent, content lab, and intelligence assistant — draw from a shared daily token budget. The budget resets at midnight UTC every day.

### Budget by plan

| Plan | Daily budget |
|------|-------------|
| Trial | 20,000 tokens |
| Starter | 20,000 tokens |
| Pro | 110,000 tokens |
| Pro Autopilot | 110,000 tokens |

### Approximate token cost per action

| Action | Estimated tokens |
|--------|-----------------|
| Marketing agent — basic insight | ~800 |
| Marketing agent — full analysis | ~12,000 |
| Management agent — implementation task | ~1,800 |
| Content lab — post generation | ~1,500 |
| Campaign content set | ~2,500 |
| Intelligence assistant — message | ~2,000 |

These are estimates. Actual usage varies based on the length and complexity of the request.

### What happens when the budget is reached

When the daily budget is reached:
- AI-powered features (marketing agent, management agent, content lab, assistant) return a limit message.
- The rest of the platform — task management, revenue tracking, integration status, existing data — remains fully accessible.
- The budget resets at midnight UTC. AI features resume automatically.

The platform displays your current usage and remaining budget in the hub so you can track consumption.

---

## Changing plans

### Upgrading
Upgrades take effect immediately. The new plan's token budget applies from the moment of upgrade.

### Downgrading or canceling
Paid plan users access billing via the Stripe billing portal (available in hub settings). Downgrade or cancel takes effect at the end of the current billing period. During the remaining period, you keep full access to your current plan.

Trial users who want to subscribe early can upgrade directly from the hub.

---

## Billing

Payments are processed by Stripe. EarnedLab does not store payment card details. Billing is monthly and charged on the date you originally subscribed.
