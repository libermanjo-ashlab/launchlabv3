# Intelligence Assistant

The intelligence assistant is EarnedLab's context-aware AI chat — available directly in your business hub. It knows your business: your offer, target customer, market, plan, goals, and recent agent activity. Ask it anything related to your business and it draws on that context to give specific, relevant answers.

---

## What the intelligence assistant knows

When you open a chat session, the assistant has access to:

- **Your business profile** — Name, description, offer, pricing, target customer, and positioning.
- **Your market validation** — The customer problem, competitive landscape, and market analysis from your validation run.
- **Your business plan** — Offer structure, milestones, and task list.
- **Your goals** — Revenue targets, timeline, and constraints you set during discovery.
- **Your marketing history** — The most recent marketing analyses and their recommendations.
- **Your integration status** — Which channels are connected and what the management agent has recently done.

The assistant does not have access to private financial data or credentials.

---

## What you can ask

The intelligence assistant is most useful for:

**Strategy and positioning**
- "What's the strongest angle to differentiate my consulting service from competitors?"
- "Should I position myself as a specialist or a generalist?"
- "What are the three biggest growth levers for my business right now?"

**Marketing and content**
- "Draft a LinkedIn post about my freelance copywriting service."
- "Give me five content angles for Instagram this week."
- "Write a subject line for a reactivation email to past clients."

**Business operations**
- "What should I prioritize this week based on my current plan?"
- "What tasks should I complete before running the next marketing agent cycle?"
- "How should I structure my service packages?"

**Research and analysis**
- "Who is the most important segment of my target customer and why?"
- "What are the typical objections a consulting client has before signing?"
- "How do freelance photographers typically price their packages?"

**Agent guidance**
- "What should I ask the marketing agent to focus on next?"
- "Which channels should the management agent post to first?"
- "Is my current plan missing anything important?"

---

## What the assistant is not for

- Legal, tax, accounting, or regulated professional advice — consult qualified professionals for these.
- Guaranteed market predictions or revenue projections.
- Real-time information like current stock prices, live news, or today's trending topics.

---

## How to use the intelligence assistant

1. Open your business hub.
2. Navigate to the **Intelligence** or **Assistant** tab.
3. Type your question or request in the chat input.
4. The assistant responds using your business context.

Sessions are not saved indefinitely — if you return after a long gap, provide brief context at the start of the conversation.

---

## Tips for better results

**Be specific about your situation.** The assistant knows your business profile, but adding context in your message improves the response. Instead of "write me a post," say "write me a LinkedIn post targeting accountants in their 30s who are considering going independent."

**Use it to pressure-test ideas.** "Here's my thinking on pricing — what are the weaknesses in this approach?" gets more useful output than "what should I charge?"

**Chain it with agents.** Ask the assistant what marketing angles to focus on, then run the marketing agent with that framing. Use the assistant to draft a prompt for a campaign, then use the content lab to generate the full content set.
