# Marketing Agent

The marketing agent analyzes your market, generates growth opportunities, and produces content plans and channel-specific content recommendations for your business. It runs on request (Starter and Pro) or automatically on a schedule (Pro Autopilot).

---

## What the marketing agent does

Each time you run the marketing agent, it produces a full marketing analysis for your business. The analysis covers:

- **Market snapshot** — Current competitive environment and where your business sits within it.
- **Growth opportunities** — Specific, actionable opportunities based on your business type, market, and goals.
- **Channel recommendations** — Which channels to prioritize based on where your target customers are.
- **Content ideas** — Topic angles and post briefs tailored to your brand and audience.
- **Campaign outline** — A structured campaign concept with hooks, angles, and channel-specific formats.
- **Recommended next actions** — Prioritized list of what to do next based on the analysis.

The agent uses your full business profile — offer, target customer, market, goals, and history — as context for the analysis. It doesn't produce generic marketing advice.

---

## How to run the marketing agent

1. Open your business hub.
2. Navigate to the **Marketing** section.
3. Click **Run marketing analysis**.
4. The agent runs and produces a full analysis — this typically takes 30–90 seconds.
5. Review the analysis. Sections you approve can be passed to the management agent for implementation.

Previous analyses are saved in your hub so you can track how recommendations evolve over time.

---

## Plan requirements

| Plan | Marketing agent access |
|------|----------------------|
| Trial | Up to 3 full analyses |
| Starter | Unlimited analyses (within daily budget) |
| Pro | Unlimited analyses (within daily budget) |
| Pro Autopilot | Unlimited — runs automatically on a schedule |

On **Pro Autopilot**, the marketing agent runs without manual input on a regular schedule. You configure the frequency and the types of recommendations you want it to focus on.

---

## Token usage

The marketing agent uses approximately 800–12,000 tokens per run, depending on depth:

- **Basic analysis** — ~800 tokens (quick opportunity scan)
- **Full marketing analysis** — ~12,000 tokens (complete market, competitors, content, and campaign)

These are estimated costs against the daily token budget. The daily budget resets at midnight UTC. See [plans and daily limits](https://earnedlab.com/docs/plans-and-limits.md) for full details.

---

## Passing outputs to the management agent

Marketing analyses produce recommended actions. On Pro plans, you can pass approved recommendations directly to the management agent for implementation:

1. Review the marketing analysis.
2. Select the recommendations you want implemented.
3. Click **Implement selected** — the management agent receives the tasks.
4. Review and approve the implementation plan before anything goes live.

On Pro Autopilot, the management agent can execute pre-approved action categories automatically without the review step.

---

## Content generated by the marketing agent

Each analysis includes channel-specific content briefs. These can be:

- Used as-is in the content lab to generate finished posts
- Passed to the management agent for direct publication to connected channels
- Downloaded as a report for use outside the platform
